# Creator Outreach — YouTube / Podcast

## Target Creators

Search YouTube for these terms and identify 10 channels in the 50K–500K subscriber range:
- "commander deck builder"
- "EDH deck tech"
- "commander deck help"
- "EDHREC alternative"
- "magic the gathering casual commander"

Good creator signals:
- They cover tool reviews or "how I build decks" content
- They talk about casual/bracket-aware Commander (not cEDH)
- They respond to community comments
- Recent upload activity (within last 30 days)
